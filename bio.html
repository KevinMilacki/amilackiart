Alisha Milacki is a mixed media artist from Covington, KY.
She enjoys experimenting with a wide range of
mediums - from oil pastel drawings, to water color paintings and wooden wall hangings.

Her work is about nature, emotions and all the colors she loved as a child.
She thrives when she is involved in commuinity events where she can showcase her creations.
All of her work is created in her home studio.
